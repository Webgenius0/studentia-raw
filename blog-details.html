Shopify is a great platform for hosting your e-commerce store, but it’s missing some important email marketing features. To really accelerate growth, you need a more powerful tool. Check out our list of the top email marketing apps for Shopify to find one that suits your business.

How we evaluated the Shopify email marketing platforms on this list

We spent days using the products on the list to get a real feel for what they offered and to examine their pros and cons. We also looked at Shopify’s app store to see what other people think about each one.

This evaluation considered ease of use, deliverability, service quality, and price. Only the most useful apps were highlighted in this listing.

What to look for in a Shopify email marketing app

Almost all Shopify businesses will benefit from the features listed below. All the tools on our list have one or more of them, but we found that the apps that have the features in different strengths, so we highlighted some of the top apps for each feature set.

Email automation

Automation boosts sales and productivity by sending emails proven to generate sales at optimal customer touchpoints. For example, welcome emails persuade customers to purchase your product or online store, and abandoned cart emails push people who were already ready to buy to complete their orders.

How we evaluated the Shopify email marketing platforms on this list

  • E-commerce-specific triggers like abandoned cart or product purchases
  • Automation workflows with easy drag-and-drop builder
  • Pre-built templates for common use cases and customer behavior
  • The ability to set up an automation with multiple triggers
